MULTI CONNECTION GUIDE

Unblockable, High-Speed Static IP Setup

This method ensures that your Multi connection cannot be blocked by the server. It uses residential static IPs routed through a fast-paced ISP data-center connection. Follow these steps carefully!

1 IPRoyal Configuration

1. Navigate to IPRoyal ISP Proxies.
2. Buy a proxy in the nearest location between your source country and France. Ensure you do not add any optional extras.
3. Once your order is finalized, go to your Static Residential Proxies dashboard, and select your purchased proxy from the list.
4. Change the Protocol to SOCKS5 (Note that your Port number will change).
5. Scroll down to the Whitelisted IPs section, and click + Add IP.
6. Go to whatsmyip.com (ensure your VPN is OFF!) and copy your Public IP.
7. Back on IPRoyal, paste your Public IP, and click Save.

2 IPRoyal Proxy Manager Setup

1. Download & Install the IPRoyal Proxy Manager Extension to your browser.
2. Open the extension and click Add new proxy.
3. Click the Manual Proxy button.
4. Enter any memorable name, set the protocol to SOCKS5, copy your Host and Port from IPRoyal (e.g., 78.898.101.456 & 12324) and paste them into IPRoyal Proxy Manager.
5. Connect to your new IPRoyal proxy, and navigate to google.com.
  • • If the page does not load, please ensure to follow the steps exactly as shown in Phase 1 and 2.
  • • If the page does load, you are good to go!

3 Connect to Multi Easily

1. Go to hydralux.pro with your main internet connection active.

2. Once the Parent client is logged in and ready, open the IPRoyal Proxy Manager Extension and Connect to your new proxy.

3. Press Tab to spawn your Multi.

4. Wait for the toast notification to say Connected! and have fun!

Note: If you use the same name on both cells, your IPs may get banned. If this happens, you will need to reset your main ISP and IPRoyal IPs to rejoin.
